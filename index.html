How It Works

  • Open your trust wallet or metamask.
  • Go to Dapps and search for "crypto pizza" or type "cryptopizza.com".
  • Connect your wallet.
  • Select the amount of slices you want to purchase and pay the sum in BUSD.
  • Make sure you have some BNB for fees and BUSD required.
  • After payment, a success message will appear and your slices will be displayed on your dashboard.
  • Now you are ready to benefit from whatever tokens we purchase.
  • Whenever we partake in any presale, your tokens will be sent directly to your wallet and you can choose to sell them at your desired time or do with them whatever you please.
  • The token distribution will be based on the number of slices you have as displayed on your dashboard.
  • For record purpose, every token we send to your wallet will be displayed on your dashboard and the appropriate smart contract will be displayed as well.
